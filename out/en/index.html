Our services

Custom cutting

We cut logs into boards, beams and battens to your specification. Orders are completed within one working day. Every piece measured by hand — no production-line tolerances.

Swedish timber

We import certified C24 timber from Sweden — straight, dry and ready to build with. A higher standard than domestic timber, required by most structural projects.

Impregnation

We protect wood by vacuum-pressure treatment against fungi, insects and moisture. The service is available on-site at the sawmill — ideal for garden and terrace timber.
