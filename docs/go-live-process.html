  • 2 Access to the domain panel
    Either share the login details with Phil, or be ready to paste in 2 DNS records that Phil will send you
  • 3 Confirm business details
    Exact street address, opening hours, and a Google Maps pin for the sawmill location

If you are unsure about any of these, Phil can walk you through it step by step.

What the DNS change looks like

This is what you (or Phil) will do in the domain panel. It takes about 5 minutes.
  • 1 Log in to your domain registrar (the company where you bought tartakplonsk.pl)
  • 2 Find the DNS settings page (sometimes called "DNS zone" or "domain records")
  • 3 Add or update the A record — Phil will give you the exact IP address to enter
  • 4 Add a TXT record — Phil will give you the exact text to paste in
  • 5 Save. The change takes effect within a few hours (sometimes up to 24h)

Phil will send the exact values to copy and paste. You do not need to guess anything.
